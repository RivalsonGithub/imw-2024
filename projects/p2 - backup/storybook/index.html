About


Our business is a 2d mobile visual novel gacha game. We are going to be the first daughter raising game featuring an AI dynamic personality growth system.

The game is set in a fantasy medieval world of western and eastern cultures. Players' unscripted conversation (using AI) with the daughter will dynamically affect their personality. This affects the types of events that would occur and ultimately the ending. Secondary elements include mini games, dress up and dating options to balance the experience (increase retention) and broaden the range of audience. The mini games are AI chatting, match3, fishing, farming, dancing etc.

Players will be able to do microtransactions (in-app purchase) to gacha items. These items are cosmetics items (hairstyles, dresses, appearances), consumable items (items that boost stats), pets, and access to certain love interests. We are building the game using Unity Engine. The game is planned to be released on App store and Google Play.
